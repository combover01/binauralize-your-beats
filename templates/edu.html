Binauralize Your Beats is designed to improve your mood by adding binaural basslines to your favorite songs

Binauralized Audio is when 2 closely spaced frequencies are played in different ears to create a beating effect. This auditory phenomena has been shown in multiple studies to improve mood in several ways.

Benefits of Binauralized Basslines


Sleep: Frequencies ranging from 0.5 to 4 hz can improve and induce sleep
